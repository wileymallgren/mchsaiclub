About Us

The MCHS AI Club is a community of students passionate about exploring the world of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

We meet regularly to learn about cutting-edge technologies, work on exciting projects, and share our knowledge with others. Whether you're just starting out or already building AI models, our club is a place to grow and collaborate.
